Mets 3D Warehouse Viewer

Introducing the Mets 3D Warehouse Viewer, a platform that offers a three-dimensional view of a warehouse. Users can engage with the layout by moving their cursor over 3D pallet racks, which display the position per row and availability within the racks. Hovering over an accordion element will highlight the specific 3D pallet. This tool provides effortless access to comprehensive warehouse details, enabling tracking of pallet locations and quantities.

Mets 3D Warehouse Editor

Introducing the Mets 3D Warehouse Editor, a tool that presents a three-dimensional representation of a warehouse. Users have the ability to construct and modify virtual warehouse layouts, including plants and rooms. The editor provides various tools such as wall creation, rack configuration, erasing, and camera view resetting. It enables users to input warehouse information and racks configuration to generate a 3D model of the warehouse. Additionally, this editor facilitates the modification of pre-existing warehouse layouts.
